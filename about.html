About the Common Service Center

Our Mission

The Common Service Center (CSC) scheme is one of the mission mode projects under the Digital India Programme. We aim to provide high-quality and cost-effective e-governance, social, and private sector services in the areas of agriculture, health, education, entertainment, banking, and financial services to rural citizens of India.
